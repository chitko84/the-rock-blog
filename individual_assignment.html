Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson -

Dwayne Johnson, known as "The Rock," is an American actor, producer, and retired wrestler. Born on May 2, 1972, Johnson initially gained fame in the World Wrestling Federation (WWF, now WWE) as a charismatic and highly popular wrestler. He transitioned to Hollywood, becoming one of the highest-grossing and most recognizable actors, known for his roles in blockbuster franchises such as "Fast & Furious" and "Jumanji." His larger-than-life persona, combined with his versatility and work ethic, has made him a beloved figure in both sports and entertainment industries.



Here is a famous quote from Dwayne Johnson:

"Success isn't always about greatness. It's about consistency. Consistent hard work leads to success. Greatness will come. Dream big, stay positive, work hard, and enjoy the journey. The road to success and greatness is always paved with consistent hard work. Outwork your competition, be authentic, and above all else, chase your greatness."

-Dwayne Johnson



Popular Movies Awards and Acheivements
The Scorpion King (2002)
The Scorpion King was a commercial success, grossing approximately $165 million worldwide against a budget of $60 million.
WWE Triple Crown Champion: Achieved by winning the WWE Championship, Intercontinental Championship, and Tag Team Championship
Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle (2017)
The film's blend of humor, action, and nostalgia made it a box office hit, grossing over $962 million worldwide.
WWE Championships
  • 8-time WWE Champion
  • 2-time WCW/World Champion
  • 2-time Intercontinental Champion
  • 5-time Tag Team Champion
San Andreas (2015)
The film showcases Johnson's action-hero persona, grossing over $474 million globally.
Time 100: Named one of Time magazine's 100 Most Influential People in the World multiple times
Moana (2016)
The film was praised for its beautiful animation, catchy music, and engaging story, grossing over $643 million worldwide.
Hollywood Walk of Fame: Honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame (2017)
Rampage (2018)
The film features thrilling monster battles and extensive special effects, grossing approximately $428 million globally.
Forbes Highest-Paid Actor: Topped Forbes' list of highest-paid actors multiple times (2016, 2019, 2020)
Central Intelligence (2016)
The film's mix of humor and action was well-received, earning over $217 million worldwide.
NAACP Image Awards: Outstanding Actor in a Comedy Series (2017, "Ballers")
